Potential of Radar

Limitations of Traditional Radar

For decades, commercial radars have suffered from poor angular resolution and limited FOVs because traditional designs require more antennas for higher resolution. Additional antennas increase cost, size, and power exponentially, limiting what is commercially feasible.

Oculii's Virtual Aperture Imaging Technology

Oculii's Embedded AI Software enhances the resolution of any radar hardware platform by up to 100X, without increasing the physical number of antennas. Embedded in standard market proven Radar DSP processors, Oculii's Virtual Aperture Imaging Software requires no additional power or compute.
